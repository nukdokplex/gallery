# Control (Remedy Entertainment, 2019)

Third-person action-adventure. You play as Jesse Faden, director of the
Federal Bureau of Control (FBC), a secret US agency studying paranormal
phenomena. Set in the "Oldest House" — a Brutalist, shape-shifting building
with ever-changing architecture.

Known for:

- Destructible environments and telekinetic combat
- Metroidvania-style exploration
- Surreal atmospheric storytelling (live-action cutscenes)
- Multiple Game of the Year awards
- Stunning visuals — photomode captures the Oldest House, Astral Plane,
  Hiss encounters, and altered items/entities
